VitalMedicals is developing a smart software ecosystem for the healthcare industry to improve operational efficiency and patient safety.

The VitalMedicals ecosystem delivers locationally-aware, contextually-relevant information to clinicians' wearable and smart devices.

VitalsOnGlass, our first product, streams live vital signs to the operating surgeon's Google Glass during conscious sedation procedures or to the nurse in the recovery room.

SurgStream streams live fluoroscopy, ultrasound or endoscopy video to Glass during procedures.
